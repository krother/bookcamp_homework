PixPro - the Art Bot

Upload a piece of art and find out what a machine learning model sees in it

How it works

The model consists of 3 Decision Tree regressors that try to predict the R, G and B color value of each pixel given its position. Because the model has been constrained by limiting the maximum depth, the generated image is a generalized version of the original art.
